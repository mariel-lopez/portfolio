Hi, hello, kamusta!

My why — Growing up between Manila and Toronto taught me early that context shapes everything: How people navigate space, make decisions, and what "intuitive" actually means.

I bring both of those worlds into every product I work on.

My specialty — I specialize in the full arc of product design: UX, UI, research, systems, strategy, and accessibility. I care about driving the details to create the full big picture.

Outside of work — I'm usually in motion: Hosting a design workshop, taking photos on my camera, hopping on a plane, or sitting in a café with my friends.

I love bringing people together, discovering things I don't know, and finding joy in how things work.

Lately — I've been exploring First Principles Thinking and the Japanese approach to design; two ways of seeing the world that keep making my work sharper.

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.

— Steve Jobs
